> 「Tī lí-kái, tī-lō͘ kap ôaⁿ ê sio-khì pun-chú sióng tiāu ê ha̍k-li̍p.」

## Ki-pún kong-liông

Pêng-á ê ôaⁿ kóng ê sî, í-siūn sī \((x-a)^2 + (y-b)^2 = r^2\)，chia ê \((a,b)\) sī ôaⁿ-sim, \(r\) sī bán-té. Tī-lō͘ ê thò·-hoat kóng ê sî, thêng-tâi kā i thêng-kàu siunn-beh sī \(y = mx + c\) chia ê sio-tsit-hoat, líng-tâng ē-sái sī it-pang ê \(Ax + By + C = 0\).

Beh chai tī-lō͘ kap ôaⁿ ê sio-tsāi, tō ē thê-tī tī-lō͘ ê hoat-sut teh khì ôaⁿ ê hoat-sut, tn̄g tī siong pâng tsò tsi̍t-ê jî hû tshian-pó·:

\[
  (1 + m^2)x^2 + 2(m(c-b) - a)x + (a^2 + (c-b)^2 - r^2) = 0.
\]

- Nā 判別式 \(\Delta > 0\)，tio̍h ū nn̄g ê kau-tông (siong-kau).
- Nā \(\Delta = 0\)，tio̍h ū tsi̍t ê kau-tông (tshiânn).
- Nā \(\Delta < 0\)，tio̍h bē kau-tông (li-khì).

Nā tī-lō͘ siōng tsiânn sī it-pang ê hoat-sut, chia-ê huat-tō͘ mā sī tsâm-tsip ê jî hû, thêng hāu kah 判別式.

## Hoat-to chi̍t ê kun-khì

Nā tī-lō͘ sī it-pang hoat-sut, ôaⁿ-sim kàu tī-lō͘ ê kun-khì sī

\[
  d = \frac{|Aa + Bb + C|}{\sqrt{A^2 + B^2}}.
\]

拿 \(d\) kā bán-té \(r\) pī-kap:

- \(d < r\) sī siong-kau.
- \(d = r\) sī tshîng tshiat.
- \(d > r\) sī li-khì.

## Pong-lí: tshiau kau-tông

Khì-kong ôaⁿ \((x-1)^2 + (y+2)^2 = 25\) kap tī-lō͘ \(3x - 4y - 20 = 0\).

1. Tsuì tī kun-khì: \(d = |3 \cdot 1 - 4 \cdot (-2) - 20| / 5 = 3\)，nâ \(r = 5\)，siong-kau.
2. Tī-lō͘ khuànn-koh sī \(y = \frac{3x-20}{4}\)，tn̄g tī ôaⁿ:

\[
  (x-1)^2 + \left(\frac{3x-20}{4} + 2\right)^2 = 25.
\]

3. Sio-liām koh tó tsi̍t-ê hoat-sut \(25x^2 - 170x + 289 = 0\)，thiau-tok \(x = 4\) kap \(\frac{289}{25}\).
4. Koh tn̄g tī tī-lō͘, kau-tông sī \((4, -2)\) kap \((\frac{289}{25}, -\frac{47}{25})\)。

## Lian-si

- Tio̍h tī-lō͘ \(y = x + 1\) kap ôaⁿ \(x^2 + y^2 = 10\) tsuân tsi̍t tsuân, chai siong-tsāi kap kau-tông.
- Tsiok tsi̍t tia̍p tī \((2,3)\) ê tī-lō͘, ūn-sûn kap ôaⁿ \((x-3)^2 + (y-1)^2 = 13\) tshîng tshiat.
- Tńg \(x = k\) kap it-bān ôaⁿ \(x^2 + y^2 = 1\) kau-tông ê sò͘-liông, thâi-lūn參數 \(k\) ê tiâu-kiàn.
